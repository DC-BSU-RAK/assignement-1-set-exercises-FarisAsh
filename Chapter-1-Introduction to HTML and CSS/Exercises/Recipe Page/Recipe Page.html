Creamy Garlic Pasta

An incredibly easy recipe for a Creamy Garlic Pasta. Packed with flavour and you can even add scallops or prawns for a seafood alfredo.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 200g of spaghetti
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper for taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (You can also use Garnish)
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta in a large pot of boiling water and add salt. Drain it and then set it aside.
  2. Melt the butter in a skillet with medium heat. Add the minced garlic for about 1 minute until it gives off a fragrance.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream and bring it to a gentle simmer. Let it cook for 3 to 4 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the grated Parmesan cheese and stir it until it is fully melted and the sauce gets slightlty thickened.
  5. Season it with salt and pepper for taste.
  6. Toss the cooked pasta in the sauce until it is adeqautely applied.
  7. Serve and sprinkle some chopped parsely for added taste.
